About Us

Jai Rajendra Jewellers has been a trusted name in fine jewellery for over 20 years in Peddapuram. Founded by the late Sukhraj Indramailji, the business is now proudly run by his sons, Vikas Kumar and Sanjeev Kumar. We specialize in crafting timeless designs with a perfect blend of tradition and modern elegance.

Every piece we create is a reflection of our dedication to quality, craftsmanship, and customer trust. Whether it’s a delicate gold ring or an elaborate diamond set, our designs aim to make your moments truly special.

Our Story

What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most respected jewellery showrooms in the region. Our customers are not just clients—they are part of our extended family. We take pride in delivering excellence in every interaction, ensuring you leave with more than just jewellery—you leave with joy.

A Legacy of Trust

Founded on integrity, quality, and passion, our journey began over a century ago. We craft exquisite jewelry that celebrates life's most precious moments.

Our commitment extends beyond craft. We build lasting relationships with clients, guided by a tradition of trust and personalized service.

Our Vision

To be more than jewellers; keepers of tradition and innovators of style. We create meaningful pieces that celebrate life's special moments, blending timeless elegance with modern design. Every piece reflects our commitment to quality, trust, and lasting memories.
